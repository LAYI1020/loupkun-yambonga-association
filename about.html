About Us

The Loupkun Yambonga Association Inc. is focused on improving the lives of youth and vulnerable groups in the Lower Kagul, Upper Kagul, and Upper Nebilyer districts of Papua New Guinea.

We empower individuals through educational programs, skill development, and sustainable initiatives, helping to create lasting change.

Founded by community leaders including Cr. Winis Alinga, we prioritize collaboration to fulfill our mission effectively.

Providing assistance to orphans and support for schooling ensures that every child has access to quality education and brighter futures.

Additionally, we engage in agricultural projects that enhance local food security while promoting economic opportunities for families.

Through outreach programs, we foster a sense of belonging and support, enriching our community and nurturing the next generation.
