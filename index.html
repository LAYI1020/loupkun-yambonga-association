Welcome to the Loupkun Yambonga Association

The Loupkun Yambonga Association Inc. is dedicated to fostering a sustainable and united community in the Lower Kagul and Mt. Giluwe LLGs of the Tambul Nebilyer district. We aim to empower residents through shared resources and collective action.

Join us in our mission to create a supportive and thriving environment!
