Welcome to the Loupkun Yambonga Association

For Peace, Justice & Transparency, We Operate in Your Home

The Loupkun Yambonga Association Inc. supports youth, orphans, and vulnerable groups in the Lower Kagul, Upper Kagul, and Upper Nebilyer sub-districts of Papua New Guinea.

Our mission is deeply rooted in community support, providing vital resources to those in need, promoting peace, justice, and transparency.

We collaborate with local churches and organizations to facilitate effective fundraising efforts to meet community needs.

Join us in building a brighter future! We welcome your support and involvement.
